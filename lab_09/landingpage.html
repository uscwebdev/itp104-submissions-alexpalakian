THE BASICS

Gua Sha, an ancient Chinese healing technique, involves gently scraping the skin's surface with a smooth-edged tool to promote circulation and release tension. This therapy is used to improve the body's natural flow of energy, helping alleviate pain, reduce inflammation, and enhance overall well-being. Its origins date back thousands of years, making it a time-tested and effective method for rejuvenation and healing
